Projects

We engage in multidisciplinary, iterative, and evidence-based research and development with diverse stakeholders to create new technologies, activities, practices and programs for real-world impact in homes, schools, afterschool programs and online communities.




Active Research Projects

Research Projects

GenAI for K-2nd Grade Classrooms


GenAI K2 introduces early elementary school students to robotics and artificial intelligence.

GenAI for 3-5th Grade Classrooms


GenAI 3-5 introduces grade school children to robotics and artificial intelligence.

PopBots: Preschool AI Curriculum


The PopBots Platform is a tool to introduce young children to programming, robotics, and artificial intelligence by allowing them to build and program their own robots.

AI + Ethics Curriculum for Middle School


The AI + Ethics Project seeks to develop an open source curriculum for middle school students on the topic of artificial intelligence and its ethical implications.

PopPet: ScratchX Compatible Robotic AI Platform


PopPet is a simple Arduino robot originally created by Jaidyn Edwards, that children can customize and program.

Zhorai



Zhorai is an artificially intelligence conversational agent through which children grades 3-5 can learn about AI.

Embeducation


Embeducation is a web application for middle schoolers that introduces the idea of how words can be represented by features by generating word embeddings and plotting these in three-dimensional space.

Supervised Polygons


Supervised Polyons helps middle school students think critically about fairness in AI models by identifying ways that bias can manifest in algorithm-bsed decisions and exploring how this bias may impact users.

Conversational Agents


Conversational Agents uses block-based codingtools to simplify mobile app development. New blocks simplify Alexa's Skill development and neural network implementation.

The Personal Image Classifier (PIC)


PIC is an intuitive web interface hosted where users can train and test their own personalized image classification models using images taken by their web-cams.

Robotic Literacy Tools


This project, supported by a National Science Foundation (NSF) grant, is designed to develop new learning games focused on improving children’s vocabulary and early literacy skills.

Growth Mindset and Curiosity


We developed and evaluated a novel expressive cognitive-affective architecture that synergistically integrates models of curiosity, understanding of mindsets, and expressive social behaviors to advance the state-of the-art of robot companions.
