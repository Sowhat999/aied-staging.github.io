Zhorai



Zhorai was a final project for the "Democratizing AI through K12 AI Education Class" of Spring 2019. This project was developed by Jess van Brummelen, Phoebe Lin, and Galit Lukin. Zhorai is an artificially intelligence conversational agent through which children can learn about AI. By interacting with Zhorai, children get to see how machines learn and represent knowledge - and how this can sometimes lead to machines making mistakes! Zhorai was developed for students in grades 3-5.

Embeducation



Embeducation was a final project for the "Democratizing AI through K12 AI Education Class" of Spring 2019. This project was developed by Srilaya Bhavaraju, Smriti Pramanick, and Dhruvika Sahni. Embeducation is a web application that introduces the idea of how words can be represented by features by generating word embeddings and plotting these in three-dimensional space. The activity targets middle school students.

Supervised Polygons



Supervised Polygons was a final project for the "Democratizing AI through K12 AI Education Class" of Spring 2019. This project was developed by Ayesha Bajwa, Sanjana Srivastava, and Kevin Robinson. Supervised Polyons helps students think critically about fairness in AI models by identifying ways that bias can manifest in algorithm-bsed decisions and exploring how this bias may impact users. This is an activity for middle school students.
